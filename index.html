CONTEXT GUESSING
Give a sentence with an unknown word underlined or highlighted — students infer its meaning from context. Builds reading comprehension and vocabulary acquisition skills.
Example: "오늘 날씨가 흐려서 우산을 가져왔어요. What does 흐려서 mean?"
